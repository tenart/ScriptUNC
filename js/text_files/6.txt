<h1>Challenge 1</h1>

<br>

Its almost exam time and you best eat so you can get that A! You need to run over to alpine bagel and grab a bagel. Do to your big fan club at Alpine, you are going to have to bring it back to the pit to eat it! Try to complete these steps all in one script.

<br>

To begin the challenge, run the following statement: 

<br>
<pre>
startChallengeOne();
</pre>
<br>

1) Moves rameses over to the student union at coordinates X = 60 Y = 36. 

<br>

2) After reaching the student union, purchase a bagel using the function: 
<br>

<pre>
rameses.purchaseAlpine();
</pre>

<br>
3) After purchasing the bagel, return to the pit at coordinates X = 52 Y = 33. 

<br>
4) Finally, after returning to the pit, call:
<br>

<pre>
rameses.eatBagel();
</pre>
<br>

to complete the challenge.
