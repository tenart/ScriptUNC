<h1>Welcome to ScriptUNC!</h1>
<br>
In this game, you will learn how to program using Javascript!
<br>
<br>
To help you get more familiar with the game, here are a few tips:
<br>
<br>
Click and drag anywhere to pan across the map.
<br>
<br>
Click on the minimap in the top left corner to recenter the screen on Rameses.
<br>
<br>
Click the <i class="fa fa-square-o"></i> buttons to collapse these windows and save space on the screen.
<br>
<br>
The code editor can be snapped to the right side of the screen for more editing space.
<br>
<br>
Try exploring the map for a bit, and when you're ready to continue, click on the <i class="fa fa-chevron-right"></i> button to see the next lesson!
